UCSD VGDC

Who are we?

We are a community of students passionate about game development at UCSD. We’re open to students of any level of experience, whether you’ve never made a game before but are interested, or you’ve got some projects and skills under your belt.

We try to promote a collaborative environment through our events, which include quarterly game jams (make a game from scratch in a weekend with a team!), weekly meetings, and guest speakers from the games industry.

Come join us in the exciting and rewarding process that is making games!

Associations

We are associated with numerous computer science, gaming, and game development groups and strongly recommend checking them out.

Triton Gaming

UCSD Student Gaming Organization

Triton Gaming is UC San Diego’s chief gaming club, focused on all things video games and esports. Triton Gaming has established itself as the basis of the gaming community in San Diego, hosting large scale events such as LANs and viewing parties, and providing a space for gamers to get to know each other.

Triton XR

UCSD Extended Reality Organization

Triton XR connects members with the XR industry through workshops, projects, and networking. Their mission is to foster a multidisciplinary community dedicated to exploring and creating Extended Reality (Virtual, Augmented, and Mixed Reality) experiences.

IGDA - San Diego Chapter

International Game Developers Association

IGDA supports and empowers game developers around the world in achieving fulfilling and sustainable careers.
